About Us

ABOUT COMPANY

Founded in 2002, FAF has grown into a premier multi-faceted organization with 25+ years of combined expertise across real estate, food, and automobile industries worldwide. We specialize in business consultation, property management, and digital marketing services.

In 2023, FAF expanded to India with our registered office in Mangalore, Karnataka. Our team of seasoned professionals in interior/exterior design, construction, finance, and operations helps clients secure the best opportunities in real estate and construction sectors.

We provide comprehensive support including legal documentation, financing assistance, and post-registration formalities, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free experience for every client.
